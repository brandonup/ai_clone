How to use Google Drive for documents (Optional):

  1. Create a folder in your Google Drive and add your documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT).
  2. Right-click the folder and select "Share" > "Share".
  3. In the "Add people and groups" box, paste the following email address:
    ragie-drive-reader@prototype-app-456908.iam.gserviceaccount.com
  4. Ensure the permission level is set to "Viewer".
  5. Click "Send" or "Share" (no notification needed).
  6. Copy the URL link of that shared folder.
  7. Paste the folder URL into the "Google Drive Folder Link" field above.

If you provide a link, the documents will be processed after you click "Create Clone".
